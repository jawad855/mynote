How to Use Text Note Web Application ?

Learn how to effectively use the Text Note web application for creating and managing your notes. This guide covers features like saving notes, changing text styles, adding links, uploading media, and more.

...

If you're looking to streamline your note-taking experience, the Text Note web application is here to help. This guide will walk you through the various features and functionalities of this application, making it easier for you to create and manage your notes effectively.

1. Getting Started

Upon opening the Text Note web application, you'll notice a user-friendly interface designed for seamless note-taking. Here's how to get started:

Note Title: Begin by entering a title for your note in the "Note Title" input field.

Note Content: The main text area below the title is where you can write your note. It's a rich text editor that allows you to format your text, add media, and more.

2. Saving Your Notes

Text Note allows you to save your notes for future reference. To save a note, follow these steps:

Enter your note title and content.

Click the "Save Note" button. Your note will be saved locally within the application.

3. Downloading Notes

You can easily download your notes as text files. Here's how:

Enter your note title and content.

Click the "Download Note" button. This will download your note as a text file for offline access.

4. Sharing Notes

Text Note also lets you share your notes with others. To share a note, do the following:

Enter your note title and content.

Click the "Share" button. This will generate a shareable link that you can copy and send to others.

5. Styling Your Notes

Customize the appearance of your notes using these styling options:

Change Background Color: Click the "Change Background Color" button to choose a background color for your note.

Change Text Color: Use the "Change Text Color" button to modify the text color within your note.

Change Font: Click the "Change Font" button to apply different fonts to your text.

6. Adding Links

You can easily insert clickable links within your notes:

Highlight the text you want to link.

Click the "Add Link" button and enter the URL. The selected text will become a clickable link.

7. Uploading Media

Text Note supports image and video uploads for multimedia-rich notes:

Click the "Upload Media" button to select and insert images or videos into your note.

8. Note History

Text Note keeps track of your saved notes. To access your note history:

Scroll down to the "Note History" section, where you'll find a list of your saved notes.

You can load or delete saved notes from this list for easy retrieval or management.

9. Word and Letter Count

Keep track of your note's length with the word and letter count display:

As you type, the application will show you the number of words and letters in your note.

10. Additional Features

Color Options:

Customize text or background color using the color dropdown menu.

Copy Shareable Link:

Easily copy the shareable link to your clipboard by clicking the "Copy Link" button.

With these features and functionalities, the Text Note web application offers a comprehensive note-taking experience. Start using it today to enhance your productivity and organization.
